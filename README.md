# 🗓️ Weekly Time Tracker

A minimal web app to track how I spend my time during the week and make sure I stay aligned with my priorities.

## 🎯 Purpose

The goal is simple:
**be intentional with time instead of wasting it.**

This tracker helps me:

* Stay accountable
* Follow a weekly time budget
* Earn leisure time instead of defaulting to it

---

## ⏱️ Time Budget

Each week is guided by a clear structure:

### 📚 Study Session

* **Target:** 8.5h
* Auto-tracked from schedule checkboxes

### 🥊 Exercise

* **Target:** 4+ sessions
* Muay Thai · Kung Fu · Tricking (estimated)

### 📖 Reading

* **Target:** 3.5h
* ~30 min/day
* Focus on books over feeds

### 🧑‍🤝‍🧑 Socializing

* **Target:** 3h+
* Real conversations, not chats

### 🎮 Gaming / Passive Leisure

* **Limit:** ≤ 3h
* Only allowed after productive goals are met

### 🚫 Doom-scrolling

* **Target:** 0h
* No mindless consumption

---

## ✅ How It Works

* Weekly schedule with **checkboxes**
* Completed tasks automatically contribute to totals
* Visual feedback on whether I’m respecting my time budget

---

## 🧠 Philosophy

> Time is a resource.
> If I don’t control it, something else will.

This project is not about perfection — it's about **awareness and adjustment**.
