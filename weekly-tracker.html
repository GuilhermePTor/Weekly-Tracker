Core Principles

01
Study is non-negotiable — even 30 min of focused work beats 2 hours of distracted browsing. Show up daily.
02
Leisure after work on free evenings is mandatory — it's not laziness, it's recovery. Without it, everything degrades.
03
Gaming and passive rewards only unlock after reading + study goals for the day are checked off.
04
Lunch break: eat first (30 min), then study (30 min), then real break away from screens (30 min). Don't skip the break.
05
Morning study (before 9am) is optional — only do it if you arrived early and feel sharp. Never force it at the cost of Muay Thai or sleep.
06
Weekends are your long study sessions. Protect the morning 1h and afternoon 1h. These are the hardest topics of the week.
07
Socializing (quality time with people you care about) counts as productive time — it's not wasted.
08
Reading before sleep instead of screens. Even 20 pages counts. The habit compounds faster than you think.

Time Budget Rules

→
Study Session: 8.5h/week target. Spread across 7 days. Never crammed.
→
Reading: 3.5h/week (30 min/day). Books over feeds. Nonfiction + occasional fiction.
→
Exercise: Already built in (Muay Thai, Kung Fu, Tricking). Don't count walking the dog.
→
Socializing: 3h/week minimum. Real conversations, not group chats.
→
Gaming/passive leisure: ≤ 3h/week. Only after daily productive goals are met.
→
Doom-scrolling: Target 0. Every minute here is stolen from something that matters.
